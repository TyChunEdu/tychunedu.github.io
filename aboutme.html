About Me

Hi, I’m Tyler, a 4th year CS and Music with Concentration in Music Technology major at Northeastern University. I have experience in computer languages such Java, C#, SQL, C++, C, Python, JavaScript, and HTML. I am also familiar with software such as JetBrains IntelliJ IDEA & Rider, Unity, GitHub, Unreal Engine 5, Visual Studio, PostgreSQL, Playwright, Progress Telerik Test Studio, RESTAssured, Selenium, Jira, Confluence, TestRail, JUCE, Bitbucket, Jenkins, Eclipse, Ableton Live, Max/MSP, Wwise, and Maven. I also have a lot of experience in education and customer interaction, both of which I value greatly.

I have many core interests, including music composition/production, sound design, video game development, songwriting, and flute performance. I also have interests in Spanish (Spanish & Latin music), creative writing, storytelling, reading (fantasy, sci-fi, adventure), law, and marine biology. I am excited to explore all of these interests further than ever before throughout my career.

I crave creativity and depth, and always hope to incorporate these ideas in everything I do.
