My Advice for Job Seekers

Tyler Chung

One of the most invaluable insights I have gained after going to Northeastern University is understanding the job search. I am always very appreciative of my mentors and peers who have very much helped me become where I am today.

I have been asked to give advice to many job seekers, so I have decided to create this page as a way to help my peers in standing out from the crowd. I acknowledge that this is a LOT. However, I do think that in this job market people need a lot more than they expect. It may take time, but it is all ultimately worth it in the end. I hope this is helpful.
